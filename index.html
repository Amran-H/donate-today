Our Donate Plan

We use our collected donations for various purposes to help the people affected by calamities. There are three broad categories, given below-

Food for a family

We provide food on a family basis, meaning our food packages contain enough food for one family. The food packages contain essential foods such as rice, oil, dry food, etc.

Shelter for a family

It is the toughest service that we provide. As natural calamities destroy the home of these helpless people we help rebuild their shelter. This is the toughest and most costly but not impossible.

Education for a child

One of the finest works on which we put our collected donation is education. We provide all the necessary things for a human being to help him be educated. In the toughest cases, we even built a small education center.
